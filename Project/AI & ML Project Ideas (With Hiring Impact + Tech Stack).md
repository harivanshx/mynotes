


## HR & Business Impact Projects

### 1. Employee Attrition Predictor

- **Description:** Use HR datasets to forecast churn with XGBoost or neural networks, then recommend retention strategies.
    
- **Why it hires:** Proves that you can connect ML insights to HR decisions & business impact.
    
- **Tech Stack:** `Pandas`, `Scikit-learn`, `XGBoost`, `SHAP`
    

---

### 2. Commodity Price & Demand Forecasting

- **Description:** Build a time-series model (Prophet or LSTM) using real commodity data to forecast demand & price trends.
    
- **Why it hires:** Key for retail, manufacturing & finance; showcases forecasting at scale.
    
- **Tech Stack:** `Statsmodels`, `Prophet`, `TensorFlow`
    

---

### 3. Flight Delay Prediction

- **Description:** Clean a real airline dataset & build prediction models using random forests or deep learning.
    
- **Why it hires:** Demonstrates dealing with real messy data, a common interview challenge.
    
- **Tech Stack:** `Pandas`, `Feature Engineering`, `Scikit-learn`, `Streamlit`
    

---

### 4. Reinforcement Learning for Financial Trading

- **Description:** Train an RL agent (DQN/PPO) to simulate buy/sell decisions to outperform standard benchmarks.
    
- **Why it hires:** Advanced ML + finance = tier-1 skill demonstration.
    
- **Tech Stack:** `Gymnasium`, `Stable-Baselines3`, `TensorFlow/PyTorch`
    

---

### 5. Speech Emotion Recognition

- **Description:** Classify emotions from audio using CNNs or transformer-based audio models.
    
- **Why it hires:** Multimodal AI demand is rapidly increasing.
    
- **Tech Stack:** `Librosa`, `PyTorch`, `Hugging Face Transformers`
